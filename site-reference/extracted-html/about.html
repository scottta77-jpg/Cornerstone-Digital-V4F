FAQ

Frequent

Questions

Clear answers to the common queries about our services.

What services does Cornerstone Digital specialize in?

Cornerstone Digital specializes in comprehensive brand identity design, high-end UI/UX strategy, custom web development, and data-driven digital marketing. We focus on creating cohesive digital experiences that not only look stunning but also drive measurable growth and engagement for your specific brand.

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines usually depend on scope and complexity. A standard project typically takes 1–4 weeks , while larger or more complex builds may require additional time. A detailed timeline is shared after reviewing your requirements.

Do you offer custom pricing for small businesses?

Yes. Pricing is flexible and tailored based on business size, goals, and project scope. We provide custom packages to ensure small businesses get the best value without unnecessary costs.

Will I own the full rights to the final designs?

Yes. Once the project is completed and fully paid, you receive complete ownership rights to the final approved designs and deliverables.

How do we communicate during the project timeline?

We maintain clear and consistent communication through email, chat, or scheduled call s depending on your preference. Regular updates are shared throughout each project stage.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes. We offer post-launch support and maintenance options to ensure everything runs smoothly after delivery, including fixes, updates, and minor improvements if needed.
