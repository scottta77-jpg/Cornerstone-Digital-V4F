FAQ

Frequent

Questions

Clear answers to the common queries about our services.

How is Cornerstone Digital different from other agencies?

Most agencies hand over a project and move on. We build an integrated system where your design, strategy, technology, and growth all connect and drive each other forward, not as separate deliverables but as one engine built around your business.

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Timelines usually depend on scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 3–5 days, while larger builds with AI integration may require additional time. A detailed timeline is shared after reviewing your requirements.

What does the process look like from start to finish?

We start with a free 15 minute consultation to understand your business, your goals and where the gaps are. From there we build a clear roadmap and work through the pillars. Your website and automation systems can be live within weeks. Measurable growth after 14 days as your systems bed in and your customer acquisition channels gain momentum.

Will I own everything you build for me?

Completely. Every asset, every system, and every piece of content we produce belongs to you from day one. No lock-in, no hidden dependencies.

How do you keep me updated during the project?

We keep communication simple through email, WhatsApp, or scheduled calls depending on your preference. Regular updates are shared throughout each project stage.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes. We offer post-launch support and maintenance packages to ensure everything runs smoothly after go-live, including fixes, updates, and ongoing improvements as needed.
